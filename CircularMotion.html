This is a simulation of constant circular motion.

The acceleration of particle traveling at constant circular motion can be calculated with the formula a=(v^2)/r with v standing for velocity and r standing for radius.

Angular change can be calculated using the formula w=v/r. w stands for the change in angle in terms of radians/second.
